Pink Lemonade

Pink Lemonade is a very common pink lemonade in the United States. The most interesting is that besides being delicious, because it has an extra flavor that comes from the gooseberry, it is beautiful and serves as a party drink for those who do not drink anything alcoholic.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups (355 ml) lemon juice (about 10 medium lemons)
  • 4 ½ cups (1065 ml) water
  • 2 cups (480 ml) of cranberry juice, pomegranate juice or more water
  • 1 cup (240 ml) white sugar
  • ¾ cup (180 ml) of raspberries or strawberries (fresh or frozen)
